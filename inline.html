The K.L.E Society's KLE Institute of Technology is an engineering college in Hubli, India. Established in 2008, it is one of the institutes under the banner of Karnatak Lingayat Education Society. KLEIT is approved by the AICTE and recognized by University Grant Commission of India.

The English Wikipedia is, along with the Simple English Wikipedia, one of two English-language editions of Wikipedia, an online encyclopedia. It was founded on 15 January 2001 as Wikipedia's first edition and, as of 1 September 2022, has the most articles of any edition, at 6,546,306.[1] As of September 2022, 11% of articles in all Wikipedias belong to the English-language edition; this share was more than 50% in 2003.[2][3] The edition's one-billionth edit was made on 13 January 2021.
